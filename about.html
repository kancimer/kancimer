About Photka

Photo studio Photka was founded in 2002. by world renowned photographer Marina Leovac in Zagreb.She is best known for her iconic portraits that depict women as imagined characters from film and other media. She is best known for "Complete Untitled Film Stills," a series of 70 black-and-white photographs which were meant to subvert the stereotypes of women in media.

Our mission is to provide resources and inspiration for learning photography while still letting one's unique flare stand out. We encourage you to sign up for our classes under the Education section in out navigation.
